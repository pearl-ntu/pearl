Xiaogang Liu

Associate Professor

xiaogang.liu@ntu.edu.sg

Professor Liu leads research on the rational design of functional luminophores, integrating chemical synthesis, photophysical characterization, and computational modeling. His work addresses fundamental challenges in dye chemistry and photochemistry, enabling high-performance applications in bioimaging, sensing, therapy, and photonics.
