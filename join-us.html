Join Us

Opportunities

We are always looking for motivated and talented individuals to join our research team. We welcome applications from prospective graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and visiting scholars.

PhD Students

We are seeking PhD students with a strong background in chemistry, physics, or related fields. Experience in computational chemistry, spectroscopy, or organic synthesis is a plus.

Postdoctoral Researchers

Postdoctoral positions are available for researchers with expertise in computational chemistry, photophysics, or molecular design. Competitive salary and benefits.

Visiting Scholars

We welcome visiting scholars and researchers for collaborative projects. Please contact us to discuss opportunities.
